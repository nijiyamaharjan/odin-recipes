Bhaat

Description

"Daal-Bhaat power: 24 Hour!". Make the first part of this classic Nepali dish morning, noon or night for a filling meal. Goes as it is, or with any vegetable combo.

Ingredients

  1. White Rice
  2. Water

Steps

  • Take 1 cup of rice for 1 serving.
  • Take ! cup + 1 and half cups of water.
  • Wash the rice thoroughly and drain it.
  • Put the rice and water in pressure cooker
  • Put on medium heat for 2-3 whistles.
  • Take off heat and let rest for about 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
